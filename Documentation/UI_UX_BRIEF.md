# **UI/UX Brief**

## **Project**

**NayePankh Foundation Website Redesign**

## **Design Goal**

Create a modern, trustworthy, youth-focused NGO website that feels:

* Professional  
* Inspiring  
* Modern  
* Accessible  
* Responsive  
* Action-Oriented

The design should encourage visitors to explore programs, understand the organization's impact, and take action by volunteering or contacting the foundation.

---

# **1\. Design Principles**

### **Clarity First**

Visitors should understand within 5 seconds:

* What NayePankh Foundation is  
* What it does  
* How they can participate

---

### **Trust Building**

Use:

* Impact statistics  
* Certificates & Gallery  
* Real-world imagery  
* Clean layouts

to establish credibility.

---

### **Action Driven**

Every section should naturally guide users toward:

Explore Programs  
Become Volunteer  
Contact Foundation  
---

### **Accessibility**

Design should remain readable and usable for all users.

---
